Question: What problems does earned wage access solve for workers?

Answer:
Earned wage access solves the problem of providing timely pay to gig workers and traditional employees outside the gig economy. It offers financial flexibility, allowing workers to access their earned wages when needed, helping them meet urgent financial needs without resorting to high-interest loans or credit cards. Additionally, earned wage access can attract and retain workers while providing financial security for unexpected expenses under $500.
